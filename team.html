About the Project

Keep Attendance started as an idea to solve a real, everyday problem.

Keep Attendance was built by two students who saw firsthand how much time and accuracy was lost to manual attendance tracking. Rather than accepting the status quo, they set out to apply modern AI and computer vision to build a system that could solve the problem — simply, affordably, and reliably.

The project represents a full engineering cycle: from identifying the problem and researching solutions, to designing and building a working system, testing it in real environments, and reflecting on how to improve it further.

Both co-founders contributed equally to every stage of the project — from ideation and system architecture to frontend development and testing.
